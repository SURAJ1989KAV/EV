Instructions for the User

Select EVSlot1 Tab. Use Press to Start to begin the charging system. Please take a look at the Initial Charge. Observe the time Needed for Full Charging. Choose Delayed Charging mode or Normal Charging mode. Delayed mode of charging is selected by default. Enter your leaving time in 24hr format of Hours and Minutes in case of Delayed Charging mode else switch into normal mode. Normal charging mode starts right away, and for delayed charging mode, wait till the starting time of delayed charging as shown in the status notification. View the Updated Charge % during charging and the time remaining of fully charging. Once the battery is fully charged, use Press to Stop for completing the charging process.

*Note: After Choosing any mode of charging, in case If you need to switch to another charging mode, then Select Press to Stop and restart the procedure.
